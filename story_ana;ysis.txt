IPL Data Story

The analysis of IPL matches from 2008 to 2016 reveals several important trends.

Mumbai Indians emerged as the most successful team with 80 wins, indicating consistent performance over multiple seasons.

Mumbai hosted the highest number of matches, making it a major IPL venue.

Chris Gayle received the highest number of Player of the Match awards, demonstrating his outstanding individual performance.

The seasons between 2011 and 2013 had the highest number of matches, showing the growth of the IPL tournament.

These findings help understand team success, player performance, and match distribution across cities.
